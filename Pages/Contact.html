Dipl.-Ing. Dr. Gernot Mevec

Experienced Engineer and Scientist

With over 35 years of experience in the industry, Dr. Gernot Mevec is a respected engineer and scientist. He has successfully completed his studies at both the Montanuniversität Leoben and the Technical University of Vienna. His extensive expertise ranges from research and material development to practical applications and intensive customer support across four continents.

Experience and Expertise

  • 35 years of industry experience: Dr. Mevec has an impressive career in the industry, which has taken him through various positions and projects worldwide. His expertise in material and material development has significantly contributed to innovation in numerous industries.
  • 20 years of teaching experience: In addition to his practical work in the industry, Dr. Mevec has taught at universities and higher technical institutes for over two decades. His experience as an instructor encompasses training future engineers and technicians, contributing to the development of the professional community.
  • Research, material development, and customer support worldwide: Dr. Mevec's work is characterized by its international focus. He has led and supervised research projects on four continents, developed new materials, and supported customers in various industries. His global network and interdisciplinary projects make him a top-level expert.

Founding of the Engineering Office

In 2020, Dr. Mevec founded his own engineering office with the aim of advising companies and organizations in the fields of research, development, and application of materials. His years of experience and ability to find innovative solutions make him a sought-after consultant and partner for companies around the world.

Services and Offers

  • Consultation and support in material development and damage cases
  • Research and innovation projects for companies
  • Customer support and technical assistance in the application of new materials
  • Training and further education for companies and educational institutions
